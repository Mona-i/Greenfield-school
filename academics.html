A rigorous, well-rounded curriculum designed to prepare every student for WAEC, NECO, and beyond

Our Curriculum

Six Years of Focused, Purposeful Learning

Greenfield Secondary School offers the full six-year secondary education programme in line with the Nigerian National Curriculum — three years of Junior Secondary School (JSS) and three years of Senior Secondary School (SSS).

At JSS level, all students follow a broad general curriculum. At SSS level, students choose one of three specialist streams: Science, Arts & Social Science, or Commercial.

All SSS3 students are registered for both WAEC (West African Senior School Certificate) and NECO (National Examinations Council) examinations.
