ResultsOctober 5, 2024

Greenfield Achieves Record 98% WAEC Pass Rate in 2024

Greenfield Secondary School has achieved its highest-ever West African Senior School Certificate Examination (WASCE) pass rate of 98%, with 42 students earning straight A's in at least five subjects. This outstanding performance places the school among the top 10 performing schools in the Federal Capital Territory.

The Principal, Dr. Chukwuemeka Nwosu, attributed the results to the school's focus on continuous assessment, targeted revision lessons, and the unwavering dedication of the teaching staff. Three students — Amaka Eze, Babatunde Alao, and Fatima Isah — each recorded 9 As in their WAEC results.
