About Our School

A Community Dedicated to Learning and Growth

Founded in 2002, Greenfield Secondary School has grown into one of Abuja's most respected centres for secondary education. Our WAEC and NECO results consistently rank among the best in the FCT, and our alumni have gone on to study at top Nigerian and international universities.

We combine a rigorous academic curriculum with sports, arts, and character development programmes — raising well-rounded Nigerians ready to lead.
