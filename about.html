About Greenfield Secondary School

Two decades of nurturing excellence, character and community in the heart of Abuja

Our Story

Established 2002, Growing Ever Since

Greenfield Secondary School was founded in 2002 by a group of visionary educationists who believed that Nigerian students deserved a school environment that combined academic rigour with deep respect for character and culture. What started with just 80 students and 12 staff has grown into a vibrant community of over 1,200 students and 80+ staff.

Our school is fully accredited by the Federal Ministry of Education and the Abuja Municipal Area Council (AMAC). We are registered with both WAEC and NECO, and our students consistently feature among the top performers in FCT examinations.

A Word from Our Principal

"At Greenfield, we believe that every child carries within them a seed of greatness. Our job — as teachers, mentors and guardians — is to water that seed with quality education, nurture it with love and discipline, and watch it grow into a life of impact."

I am immensely proud of what our students and staff achieve year after year. Our 2024 WAEC results, our sporting victories, our science fair innovations — these are not accidents. They are the fruit of hard work, dedicated teaching and a school community that genuinely cares.
