Frequently Asked Questions

When does the 2025/2026 academic session begin?

The 2025/2026 academic session is scheduled to begin in September 2025. The exact resumption date will be communicated to all admitted students and parents via letter and on this website.

Does the school offer boarding accommodation?

Currently, Greenfield is a day school. We do not offer boarding facilities at this time. Students are expected to commute to school daily.

How do I check my child's academic results?

Results can be accessed through the Student Portal using the login credentials issued at enrolment. Term report cards are also sent home with students at the end of each term.

Can I pay school fees in instalments?

Yes. We understand that paying full fees upfront may be difficult for some families. Please speak to the school Bursar to arrange an approved instalment payment plan. Students on payment plans must have their first instalment paid before resumption.
