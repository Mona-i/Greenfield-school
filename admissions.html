Admissions 2025/2026

Everything you need to know about joining Greenfield Secondary School, Abuja

⚠️
Application Deadline: All admission forms must be submitted by 30th October 2024. The JSS1 Entrance Examination is scheduled for Saturday, 16th November 2024. Late applications will not be considered.
Application Process

How to Apply — Step by Step

Follow these steps to apply for admission to Greenfield Secondary School

1

Obtain an Application Form

Purchase the admission form from the school's Administration Office (Plot 42, Greenfield Crescent, Gwarinpa, Abuja) between 8:00am and 3:00pm, Monday to Friday. Forms cost ₦5,000 (non-refundable). Forms are also available for download on this page.

2

Complete the Application Form

Fill in all required sections — student's personal details, parent/guardian information, previous school record, and emergency contacts. Ensure all information is accurate and legible.

3

Attach Supporting Documents

Include the following documents with your completed form: original and photocopy of birth certificate, Primary 6 school leaving certificate / JSS3 report card (for transfer students), two recent passport photographs, parent/guardian's valid ID, and proof of address (utility bill or national ID).

4

Submit Your Application

Return the completed form and all documents to the Admin Office. You will receive a Candidate Reference Number that you will use to check your examination date and results.

5

Sit the Entrance Examination

Prospective JSS1 students will write an entrance examination in English Language and Mathematics on Saturday, 16th November 2024 at 9:00am. Students seeking transfer admission to JSS2, JSS3, SSS1 or SSS2 will undergo an assessment interview.

6

Check Your Results & Receive Offer Letter

Examination results will be published on the school notice board and on this website by 30th November 2024. Successful candidates will receive an offer letter by email and post within 7 working days.

7

Pay Acceptance Fee & First Term Fees

To secure your place, pay the acceptance fee of ₦15,000 within 14 days of receiving your offer letter. First term school fees must be paid before or on resumption day. Failure to pay will result in forfeiture of admission.

Requirements

Admission Requirements

To be eligible for admission to Greenfield Secondary School, applicants must meet the following criteria:

✅
Age: JSS1 applicants must be between 10 and 13 years old as at September of the year of entry.
✅
Primary Education: Must have completed all six years of primary schooling and hold a valid Primary School Leaving Certificate.
✅
Entrance Examination: Must sit and pass the school's entrance examination in English and Mathematics.
✅
Transfer Students: Must provide at least two consecutive terms of academic results from their previous school.
✅
Conduct: Transfer students must provide a school leaving certificate that indicates satisfactory conduct and no disciplinary record of expulsion.

Greenfield Foundation Scholarship

We believe no brilliant Nigerian child should be denied quality education due to financial constraints. Our partial scholarship covers up to 40% of annual school fees for qualifying students.

Criteria: Minimum 70% average in Primary School Leaving Certificate, household income below ₦500,000/year, demonstrated need supported by two community references.
